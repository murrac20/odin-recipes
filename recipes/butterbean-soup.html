Sandra's Butterbean Soup

This recipie was shared with me with a friend of mine. It makes for a delicious lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 tin butterbeans (240g drained weight)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 shallots, diced
  • large handful fresh basil
  • 200g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 120ml vegetable stock
  • salt and pepper

Method

  1. Add olive oil to a pan on medium heat. Add in garlic and shallots cooking for 2 minutes until soft
  2. Add tomatoes to the pan and cook until they begin to soften and split, then add basil and cook
  3. Pour stock into pan and simmer for 3-4 minutes
  4. Add butter beans and simmer until heated through
  5. Season with salt and pepper
