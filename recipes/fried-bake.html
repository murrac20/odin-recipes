Fried Bake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour , plus more for dusting
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon butter or margarine- this is totally optional
  • 1 cup water, plus 1 tablespoon if needed
  • 2 cups canola or coconut oil for frying the bakes

Method

  1. Mix together flour, baking powder, sugar and salt.
  2. Add butter and mix with a fork or use your fingers until the flour resembles coarse breadcrumbs
  3. Make a hole or well in the center of the flour and start pouring 3/4 cup water while mixing and stirring in a circular motion.
  4. Add 1/4 cup more water and continue mixing gently for about 5 minutes until you form a nice soft dough.
  5. If dough is too sticky, dust with a tablespoon of flour, if dough is too dry after adding all the water, add another one tablespoon or so of water and bring the dough together.
  6. Allow to rest for about 3-5 minutes, then dust with flour and knead to form a smoother dough.
  7. Rub some oil on top of dough and allow the dough to rest for 25 - 30 minutes covered with plastic wrap or a damp paper or kitchen towel
  8. Cut the dough into 6 or 8 pieces (depending on how large you want the fried bakes to be)
  9. Roll them into balls and place on a clean, well-floured surface. Allow to rest for at least 10-15 minutes. covered
  10. Flatten the balls of dough until they are about 1/4 inch thick, and 5 -6 inches in diameter using your fingers or a rolling pin
  11. Add oil in a deep pan to a depth of about two inches over medium heat
  12. When oil is heated through, start adding the rolled out dough to the hot oil
  13. Turn dough immediately as it's added to the oil , as this will help the bake swell or puff up
  14. Fry in hot oil until both sides are golden brown, about 30 - 45 seconds per side ,turn over once
  15. Drain on paper towel so as to eliminate some of the grease from the oil.
  16. Serve with Fried Salt fish or Fried Shark or anything you fancy.
